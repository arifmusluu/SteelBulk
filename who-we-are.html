WHO WE ARE

STEEL&BULK TRADERS SHIPPING PTE. LTD. (“Steel&Bulk Shipping Ltd” or “Steel&Bulk Singapore Pte Ltd” or “Steelbulk Denizcilik Limited Sirketi”) is an international Dry Bulk Chartering-Commercial Management of vessels company established in 2015 headquartered in Singapore and Istanbul.

Steel&Bulk mainly operates in Panamax, Ultramax, Supramax, and handysize segments and provides flexible and reliable transportation services to its customers by being sure its job was done in the best way and aims to develop strong long-term industry relationships with its customers. For that, The Company has strong relationships with shipowners-Operatorsbrokers around the world that let access to a considerable size fleet of vessels.

In 2022, it is on its way to becoming a shipowner/operator company by adding Ship Management to its activities besides chartering.

The mission is to provide measurable and prime transportation services to customers by contributing to the ecological balance in accordance with the essence of the existence of the world

Steel&Bulk targets low expenses and high income in all of its businesses.
